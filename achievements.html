Our Story

Built on fieldwork.
Driven by precision.

WDA Lab was founded on a single conviction: that conservation outcomes depend as much on the quality of data behind the science as on the fieldwork itself. Operating at the intersection of wildlife research and data precision, we have become a trusted partner for conservation organizations working across some of Africa's most ecologically significant landscapes.

Our team brings together field-trained wildlife specialists and data professionals who understand both the scientific demands of conservation research and the operational realities of working in complex African ecosystems. We handle large-scale camera trap image libraries, biodiversity monitoring datasets, and multi-site data management workflows with the accuracy, consistency, and turnaround that modern conservation programs require.

Every project we deliver is a contribution to the broader mission of protecting Africa's wildlife heritage — one dataset at a time.
